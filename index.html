Introduction

My name is Abdulhameed Timan and I'm from Sudan. I went to school in my country up until I finished highschool. While I was studying at school my favourite subject was computing, however the subject didn't exisit in my school at the time. So instead I studied other subjects such as English language, Religion, Maths, Arabic Language, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. I came to England in May 2019. It wasn't easy for me to leave my country and leave behind all my family and friends. To start a new life in a new country with a different culture and language. The most diffcult thing I found in England was integrating into the community without knowing the language, in order to that i manged to go to school to learn the language. The first place I visited it was Cornerstone Day Center (Caritas) the location was near to Manchester University, I found it was an amazing place to learn English language and to have a chat with other students from different backgrounsd. I started studying with them and at the same time I was doing volunteering. I finished my studying with Cornerstone Day Center (Caritas) when I recived my Level 2 Functional Skills Certificate in English, which I received in July 2021.

Why do you want to join code your future ?

After I received my Level 2 certificate I didn't know what to do at that time, my teacher told me about Code Your Future because I told him before that I had an intrest to get skills and trainnig in computer programming. Therefore I jumped at the chance to join the course. Also I did a business entrepreneur course with the Ice Academy (Caritas), from which I graduated on the 26th of October 2021. I had my business idea which was to build software that can help parents monitor their kids' phones and computers. However, I need more knowledge of programming to help me set up my business idea and, with Code Your Fututure, I'm at the right place to learn the skills in order to set-up my business idea. Finally, whether it is my own business, or with someone else, coding is a great way for me to find employment in the future. I listened to two forme Caritas students who did Code Your Future and are now working in the tech industry. They inspired me to apply for this course.

how much effort are you willing to put in during the course?

I will do what I have to do with the course and I will use all my power, passion and determination to make it happen to achieve the skills required to get my future dream job.
